Recap of today's events

Well, the title is more promising than the actual things that happened during the day. Laura has changed rooms, she's in a single room now including a TV set with VHS video (the elders might remember this tape-based DVD format). This is probably the only good thing about being treated like an outlaw in a hospital. Evil Norovirus, evil you! Oh, and Laura gets her antibiotics in form of pills now. The other big change of the day. Are you just as moved as we are? I have passed three hours at home with Lena and Laura's family, and now I am back with Laura and Emma(!). 18:30. Two slices of bread? Check. One slice of cheese? Check. Contractions? Duh. While Laura is forced to eat the hospital food, I decided to have a D?ner Kebab at a food stand close to the hospital. I deeply regret now. Remember? I am to wear mouth protection...
